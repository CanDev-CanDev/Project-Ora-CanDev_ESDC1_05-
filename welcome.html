Project Ora

Welcome to Project Ora!

Project Ora, is a business tool developed to help Canadians determine if their company falls under federal juristiction; furthermore, data about federal companies are updated in a database.

As one of the main ways to distinguish between a provincial or federal company is their reach beyond the borders of their province, our team sought it fitting to name the project by the name of "Ora", meaning "Border" in Latin.

This tool was created during the CanadaDev Challenge at uOttawa by Kyrellos, Melissa, Ishita, and Kish.
